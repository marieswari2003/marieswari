First aid guidlines

  • Burns

    • Flush the burned area with cool running water for several minutes.

    call 911 for a severe burn.

    Apply a burn ointment or spray for pain.

    Take ibuprofen or acetaminophen for pain relief if necessary.

  • cardiac arrest

    • shake and shout.

    check for breathing

    place your hands at the center of their chest.

    push hard and fast-about twice per second.

  • Allergic reactions

    • Antibodies attach to mast cells.

    Allergen binds to antibodies on mast cell.

    Histamine is released from the mast cell.

    An allergic reaction ensues.

  • Heat stroke

    • Sponge the person with cool water.

    Fan the person while misting with cool water.

    Place ice packs or cool wet towels on the neck,armpits and groin.

    Cover the person with cool damp sheets.
